Rules

User Registration

All users must provide accurate and up-to-date information during registration, including their name, date of birth, gender, preferred genders of other people, location, email address, and contact information. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account details. In order to use the website, a user has to be 18 years old or older.

Profile Quality

Users must create comprehensive profiles with one profile picture (a clear headshot) and a select their passions from a pre-determined list. Profiles should be free from explicit content, hate speech, and false information.

Search Functionality

The dating website offers random selection of people based on preferred genders and passions configured in one's account settings.

Communication

The dating website provides no communication service. Profiles have publicly visible e-mail address for contacting them.
